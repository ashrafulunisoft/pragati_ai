## 1. PURPOSE

The Vendor agrees to receive and maintain in strict confidence certain confidential and proprietary information of UCBL for the purpose of:

- Evaluating the Request for Proposal (RFP) Reference No. VMS/UCBL/RFP/2026/001
- Developing a proposal for the Visitor Management System project
- Potentially entering into a business relationship with UCBL
- Understanding UCBL's business operations, systems, and requirements

The purpose of this Agreement is to protect UCBL's confidential information while allowing the Vendor to fulfill these objectives.

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" shall mean any and all information, data, or knowledge, disclosed, made available, or accessed by the Vendor in connection with this Agreement, including but not limited to:

**2.1 Technical Information:**
- Software architecture and design documents
- Source code and algorithms
- Database schemas and data models
- API specifications and integration points
- System configurations and settings
- Security protocols and encryption methods
- Network diagrams and infrastructure details
- Technical specifications and requirements

**2.2 Business Information:**
- Business strategies and plans
- Financial information and budgets
- Customer data and visitor information
- Operational procedures and processes
- Marketing plans and strategies
- Pricing models and cost structures
- Vendor lists and supply chain information

**2.3 Project Information:**
- Project requirements and specifications
- Project timelines and milestones
- Project budgets and cost estimates
- Risk assessments and mitigation plans
- Implementation strategies
- Testing plans and results

**2.4 Other Information:**
- UCBL's organizational structure
- Employee information and contact details
- Bank policies and procedures
- Security policies and access controls
- Any other information marked as "Confidential" or "Proprietary"

---

## 3. EXCLUSIONS

Confidential Information shall not include information that:

1. Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Vendor
2. Was already known to the Vendor prior to disclosure by UCBL without restriction of confidentiality
3. Is independently developed by the Vendor without use of Confidential Information
4. Is rightfully obtained by the Vendor from a third party without confidentiality restrictions
5. Is required to be disclosed by law, court order, or regulatory authority (provided Vendor gives UCBL prior written notice to allow UCBL to seek protective order)

---

## 4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE VENDOR

**4.1 Non-Disclosure**
The Vendor shall:
- Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose, publish, or disseminate Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent from UCBL
- Limit access to Confidential Information only to those employees, agents, or subcontractors who have a bona fide need to know and who are bound by similar confidentiality obligations
- Use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose defined in Section 1

**4.2 Security Measures**
The Vendor shall:
- Implement and maintain reasonable security measures to protect Confidential Information
- Prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure
- Use the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as it uses to protect its own confidential information
- Promptly notify UCBL in writing of any unauthorized disclosure or breach

**4.3 Copy and Storage**
The Vendor shall:
- Make no copies of Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon UCBL's request or termination of this Agreement
- Delete all electronic copies and securely destroy physical copies

**4.4 Employee and Subcontractor Obligations**
The Vendor shall:
- Ensure all employees, agents, and subcontractors with access to Confidential Information sign appropriate confidentiality agreements
- Be responsible for any breach of this Agreement by its employees, agents, or subcontractors

## 5. TERM AND TERMINATION

**5.1 Term**
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **two (2) years** from the Effective Date, or until:

- The RFQ process is completed and a contract is awarded
- UCBL notifies the Vendor that it is no longer under consideration
- The Vendor withdraws from the RFQ process

**5.2 Termination**
Either party may terminate this Agreement with **thirty (30) days** written notice to the other party.

**5.3 Survival of Obligations**
The obligations of confidentiality under this Agreement shall survive termination and continue for a period of **five (5) years** from the date of termination.
